Welding coordination

In order to respond to the needs of an increasingly demanding market, we provide:

  • Welding specialist in welding coordination in accordance with EN 1090 and ISO 3834 standards, for company certification
  • RQPS, creating qualifications for welding procedures
  • EPS's, creating specifications for welding procedures

Training in Welding Processes

We train a professional workforce that meets the needs of the national and international market. For this, we created dynamic courses, made to measure and excellent training.
  • MIG/MAG/FF 114/131/135/136/138
  • TIG 141/142
  • SER/MMA 111
In:
  • Carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum, copper, etc...
About the Courses/Trainings:
  • Flexible schedules and personalized training

  • A complete class is not necessary to start training, we practice individual training
  • Course and/or training in welding in our facilities, works or customer facilities, tailored to your needs

Consultancy

  • In ISO 3834-1 Quality Requirement for Fusion Welding of Metallic Materials
  • In the EN 1090-1/2/3 standard Execution of steel and aluminum metallic structures in different execution classes
  • Purchase of equipment
  • Process design techniques
  • Work planning and structures
  • Techniques and processes
  • Visual inspection of welding
  • Hiring of welders
